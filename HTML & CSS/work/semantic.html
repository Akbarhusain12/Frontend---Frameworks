Inception

Inception is a sci-fi thriller directed by Christopher Nolan that follows a skilled thief, Dom Cobb, who has the unique ability to enter people's dreams and steal their secrets. He is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased if he can do the impossible: plant an idea into someone's mind, known as inception. As he assembles a team to accomplish this task, they navigate layers of dreams, facing unexpected challenges and the manifestations of Cobb's troubled past.
